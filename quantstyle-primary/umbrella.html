FIDUS Finanz AG is a dynamically growing financial services company from Frankfurt am Main. The company services both private and institutional investors. The company is managed by renowned capital market experts with decades of experience, who are loyal and reliable partners to their customer

FIDUS has a total of three business areas: Asset management is aimed at demanding individual clients as well as foundations and family offices that require a truly individual strategy. The fund advisory provides tied agent services to portfolio managers wishing to set up their own investment fund. As a service provider or outsourcing company, the Compliance / AML business unit supports smaller banks and other financial service providers in fulfilling the relevant regulatory requirements in order to reduce their business risks.
